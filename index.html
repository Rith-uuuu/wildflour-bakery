Our Artisanal Baking Experience

Welcome to Wildflour Bakery! We specialize in sourdough breads, hand-rolled pastries, and signature rustic cakes. Everything we create utilizes locally sourced organic ingredients, traditional long-fermentation processes, and a lot of passion.

Sourdough Bread

Our classic country loaf features a crisp, blistered blister crust and a perfectly chewy, airy interior crumb framework.

Croissants

Flaky, golden-brown laminated layers made with pure French cultured cream butter. Rolled carefully by hand every single morning.

Rustic Tarts

Seasonal local fruits baked inside a crisp, rich sablée shortbread pastry shell crust, sweetened with raw organic honey.
