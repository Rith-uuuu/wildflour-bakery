Good to know

Common questions.

Can I order ahead for pickup?
Yes — use the order form and choose a pickup time. We’ll have everything boxed and ready, coffee freshly pulled.
Do you have gluten-free or vegan options?
We bake a rotating selection of gluten-free and vegan pastries daily, and our coffee bar has oat, almond, and soy milk.
How much notice do you need for cakes?
Whole cakes and custom celebration cakes need at least 48 hours. Larger catering orders are best with 2–3 days’ notice.
Is there seating and wifi?
Plenty of both. We have indoor and patio seating, free wifi, and outlets — a favorite spot to work or linger over a pour-over.
